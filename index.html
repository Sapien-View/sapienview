Transforming Organ Transplantation with Visual AI

Every year, tens of thousands of organs are declined for transplant due to uncertainty about their quality. Sapien View is changing that — empowering clinicians with AI-driven visual assessment tools that help make faster, more confident decisions at the point of donation.

Our Mission

To increase global transplant rates and improve patient outcomes by enabling a new standard of visual organ assessment — accessible anywhere, from any smartphone.

How it works

  • Capture - A clinician takes images of the organ at the point of retrieval.
  • Analyse – Our cloud-based AI model, built on years of expert-labelled data, assesses organ viability in real time.
  • Inform – Clinicians receive an evidence-based quality score that support decision-making before machine perfusion or transplant.

Why It Matters

Up to 30% of donor organs are never transplanted — often due to subjective assessment or time pressure.*

Sapien View turns visual uncertainty into quantitative insight, unlocking life-saving potential.

Designed with leading transplant surgeons on a global basis and the latest computer vision techniques.
